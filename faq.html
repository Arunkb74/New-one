Fans - Frequently Asked Questions
What is DELIGHT?

DELIGHT is an app through which fans can book personalized video messages/shout out messages called as DELIGHT video from their favorite celebrity.

How does DELIGHT work?

On the DELIGHT app, fans can search for celebrities, pick the celebrity from who they want to book the personalized DELIGHT video, fill out a request from specifying the receiver and the occasion for which they are booking the video. If the celebrity accepts the request, they will create a personalized DELIGHT video and send it back to the requester.Requester can share the video to the receiver and they can share with their friends as well.

How can I receive my DELIGHT video?

After the celebrity completes the personalized DELIGHT video message, you will be notified by SMS message and also through email. You can login to the app to view, download and share the video with the receiver, friends and loved ones.

How long will it take to receive my DELIGHT video?

Celebrities on the DELIGHT platform are given up to seven (7) days to fulfill the video requests. In most cases they will fulfill the requests under seven days. If the request is not completed within seven days, the credits you paid for the video will be refunded to the DELIGHT wallet in the app.

How much does it cost for a DELIGHT video?

The cost of the video varies from celebrity to celebrity. The cost of a DELIGHT video is set individually by each of the celebrity on the DELIGHT platform. Celebrities can change the price of their video at their discretion from time to time as they see fit. You will be charged the price that was in effect at the time you booked the DELIGHT video.

Is it guarnteed that the celebrity will make the video that I requested?

All celebrities on our DELIGHT platform have the option to accept or decline any video request that comes to them. Celebrity will have the sole discretion to accept or deny the request for the DELIGHT video. We will make every effort, that all of our customers that booked DELIGHT video with a celebrity on our platform will receive their DELIGHT video. It is also possible that your request may expire after seven days from the date of order, in which case the celebrity will not be able to fulfill your request. By using the DELIGHT platform, you agree that you may not request the celebrity to create violent, nude, partially nude, unlawful, discriminatory, hateful, pornographic, sexually suggestive or other content that is deemed inappropriate. You can check our complete Terms of Service for additional information.

How can I be informed when new celebrities join the DELIGHT platform?

You can be notified by 3 ways when celebrities join the DELIGHT platform. You can subscribe to our opt-in messages through the phone, email and newsletter. Please check our complete Terms of Service and Privacy policy for further information.

Is my video shared on the DELIGHT platform by the celebrity?

Celebrities have the sole right on the DELIGHT video that they make for you as per the request you submit through our platform. We suggest that the celebrities refrain from sharing the video on DELIGHT platform until after the event date. It is totally up to the celebrities’ discretion to share the video on the DELIGHT platform, including the timing of when they want to share the video on DELIGHT platform. As a customer you can download, share the video to the receiver and your friends on social media as many times as you want. For additional information regarding the license of the DELIGHT video please check our complete Terms of Service

Will I get to keep my DELIGHT video?

Yes, you can keep your DELIGHT video that you received from the celebrity, you can download the video, share the video to your friends on social media as many number of times as you want. You have a non-commercial and personal license to use the DELIGHT video forever, subject to the Terms of Service.

I live outside of India. Can i still order a DELIGHT video from my favorite celebrity?

Yes, you can still order a DELIGHT video from your favourite celebrity even though you may not be living in India. As long as you have an accepted payment method accepted by the DELIGHT platform you can place an order for the DELIGHT video from anywhere in the world.

Can I update my DELIGHT video request after I already booked?

Yes, you can edit, update or cancel your request for a brief period of time after your initial order of the said video. Once the celebrity records the video and sends it back to the customer, we cannot have the celebrity incorporate the changes that you may want to.

WHat are DELIGHT credits?

DELIGHT credits are used to buy DELIGHT video from the celebrity. You can buy DELIGHT credits in the app, using a valid credit/debit card, the credits will be automatically added to your wallet. Once the credits are added in your app wallet, you can then start using them to buy DELIGHT video(s) from celebrities.

How can I buy DELIGHT credits?

DELIGHT credits are sold in in packs, the price of each pack is listed in the DELIGHT app. The bigger the bundle you buy the better the discount you will get.

Will my DELIGHT credits expire?

DELIGHT credits will not expire. DELIGHT platform reserves the right to discontinue the usage of credits at our discretion at any time.

Are my DELIGHT credits refundable?

DELIGHT credits are non-refundable. Once you make a purchase of the credits they will be added to your wallet. You can use these credits at any time of you choosing to book a personalized video message from a celebrity.

I didn't receive my DELIGHT video, but my credit card has already been charged to load DELIGHT credits in my wallet, what can I do now?

If your video is booked and you did not receive the video from seven days after you booked the DELIGHT video for various reasons like cancellation of your request by the DELIGHT platform or denial of your request by the celebrity, do not worry, DELIGHT will refund the credits to your wallet. You can reuse the credits in your wallet anytime for booking videos. However, if your DELIGHT video is booked and it is past seven days since the date you booked, but you did not receive the video. Please contact our team at info@itsdelight.com
